## **PRESENTATION NOTES:**

### **Delivery Tips:**
1. **Speak to one person** - Use "you" not "sellers" - make it personal
2. **Pause after pain points** - Let them feel the struggle
3. **Show genuine excitement** at the solution reveal
4. **Use real names and stories** when possible
5. **End with urgency** but not pressure

### **Visual Design Notes:**
- Use warm, relatable imagery
- Show actual Instagram screenshots
- Use before/after comparisons heavily
- Keep text minimal, let images tell the story
- Use Nepali business examples and faces

### **Emotional Arc:**
1. **Empathy** (Slides 1-4): "I understand your struggle"
2. **Hope** (Slides 5-6): "There's a better way"
3. **Proof** (Slides 7-13): "This actually works"
4. **Logic** (Slides 14-15): "This makes sense"
5. **Urgency** (Slides 16-18): "Don't wait"
